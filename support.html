Frequently Asked Questions

Does the app work offline?

Yes, once you've downloaded the app and its content, you can use it without an internet connection. However, to receive updates and sync your progress across devices, an internet connection is required.

Which Databricks certifications does the app cover?

We cover all of the available Databricks certifications as of 2025, including:

  • Generative AI Engineer Associate
  • Machine Learning Associate
  • Machine Learning Professional
  • Data Engineer Associate
  • Data Engineer Professional
  • Data Analyst Associate
  • Developer for Apache Spark Associate
