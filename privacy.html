Privacy Policy

How we handle your data and protect your privacy

Last Updated: March 31, 2025

Introduction

We, Dipendu and Aayushi, the sole developers and publishers of the Databricks Exam app, always value, respect, and understand the importance of your privacy.

Our Privacy Commitment

We do not collect any of your personal information through our apps. Even though your learning is personalized, your identity is still anonymous to us. We uniquely identify you with a user id generated by the system and have no information linked to you or your device.

Future Plans

There's a plan to integrate app analytics in the future, but we'll update this privacy policy when we're ready to roll it out.

How We Use Your Information

Since we don't collect personal information, we only use the anonymous system-generated ID to:

  • Track your progress within the app
  • Provide personalized learning experiences
  • Save your quiz results and performance data locally on your device

Data Storage

All your app data is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit or store your data on remote servers.

Children's Privacy

Our App is suitable for users of all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information, there are no specific privacy concerns related to children's use of our app.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time, particularly when we implement analytics features. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
